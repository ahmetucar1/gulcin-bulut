Gülçin Bulut graduated in 2020 from the Department of Psychology at Aydın Adnan Menderes University. During her undergraduate studies, she completed her internship at the Psychiatry Unit of Aydın Adnan Menderes University Hospital.

After graduation, she worked with children with special needs and their families at a private rehabilitation center; conducted sessions especially focused on **Acceptance and Commitment** approaches, and delivered various trainings. She also actively provided motivation trainings for institution staff and instructors.

## Education & Experience

- Aydın Adnan Menderes University, Psychology (B.A., 2020)
- Aydın Adnan Menderes University Hospital, Psychiatry Unit (Internship)
- Counseling work with children and families at a private rehabilitation center
- Motivation trainings for staff and instructors

## Areas of Focus

Since 2020, she has been providing professional psychological counseling in the following areas:

- Relationship difficulties
- Coping with uncertainty
- Self‑confidence issues
- Burnout
- Communication problems
- Stress management
- Separation, loss and grief
- Procrastination
- Decision‑making skills
- Feelings of inadequacy

## Approach

To strengthen her professional expertise, she has completed theoretical and professional training programs in **cognitive and behavioral approaches**, **play‑based practices**, **assessment and observation‑focused approaches** used in counseling with children, and **post‑trauma adjustment and resilience processes**. She also attended an internationally standardized training program focused on **emotion processing and restructuring**.

## Volunteering & Social Support

After the February 2023 earthquakes, she volunteered in **Aegean Region Psychosocial Support** initiatives, participating in ongoing work and trainings and providing volunteer‑based psychological counseling and support sessions for individuals affected by the earthquake.
